1. Get the Solutions


To make this dye, we will need potassium ferrocyanide and iron sulfate. Prepare two cups, one for iron and the other for potassium ferrocyanide. Dissolve them in water to get the solutions.














2. Adding the Two Solutions Together


Pour the solution from one cup into another, and you will see immediate color change from green to dark blue. Then in the cup, there will be large cump of insoluable material.















3.Filtration


Get the filtration system ready and filter the solution. Then you will get a compound of a large black goop.
















4. Take off the Compound


Use a spoon to take off the compound on the filter paper into a watch glass.















5.Dry the Compound


Leave the compound on dry and cool place for a few days, and you will get a dry material that doesn't stick to the watch glass.














6.Grind the Material


Take the compound off the watch glass. Use mortal and pestle to grind the material into smooth powders, which is your prussian blue powder. You can further dissolve it into water to dye things.
